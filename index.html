Community Infrastructure Projects

Building the foundations for a secure and prosperous future through strategic, high-impact investments.

Flagship Project: Affordable Housing, Hotel & Casino

A cornerstone of our 10-year plan, this integrated project will provide 2,000 affordable housing units alongside a 200-room hotel and casino. This investment creates jobs, boosts tourism, and generates sustainable revenue to fund future community programs, all equipped with state-of-the-art security and technology.

Community Transport Initiative

Our transport initiative addresses critical mobility needs, ensuring access to education and supporting effective governance in our community.

School Commuter Bus

Ensuring every child has safe and reliable transport to school, eliminating distance as a barrier to education and promoting consistent attendance.

Traditional Council Bus

Supporting our community leaders with dedicated transport to facilitate governance, stakeholder engagement, and cultural preservation efforts.

Dr. Charles Odame-Ankrah

Dr. Charles Odame-Ankrah is a visionary scientist, award-winning inventor, and transformative community leader. He is the Principal Scientist at Direct Optics Inc., where he develops groundbreaking air quality measurement technologies. His inventions include the commercialized Photolytic Converter and Self-Aligned High-Finesse Optical Cavity.

A recipient of numerous honors, including the Queen Elizabeth II Golden Jubilee Medal, Dr. Odame-Ankrah co-founded the Calgary African Community Collective (CACC) and led the historic setup of the Ghana House in Calgary. His work bridges scientific innovation with profound social impact, empowering African and Black communities.

Ofosuhemaa Donkor

Ofosuhemaa Donkor is a seasoned CEO, investor, and serial entrepreneur with a passion for creating economic growth and jobs across Africa. As the Co-founder and CEO of a successful group of companies, she has created over 750 jobs by focusing on strong organizational governance and a culture of performance.

Ofosuhemaa excels in overall business operations, from product development and marketing to logistics. Her expertise spans Logistics & Facility Management, biometrics technology, transportation, and Project Management, driving her mission to turn African challenges into opportunities.

Robert Micah

Bobby J, as he is popularly known, is a prodigiously hardworking man with exceptional business acumen.

He has been a freelance journalist, a teacher, a radio and television host and an advocate for fairness and community justice. He has won several local and international awards including the prestigious Queen Elizabeth II Golden Jubilee Medal for community awards.

Previous positions he has held includes but not limited to Chairman of the Board, International School of Excellence (Canada), president, Ghanaian Canadian Association of Calgary, Ghanaian Times National Community Award, Executive Director of The Taradale Group of Mgmt Constituency Inc and the President and CEO of the Robert Micah Holdings Ltd to mention a few.
